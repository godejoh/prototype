Hatrick — feature walkthrough

A two-sided creative-services marketplace: customers discover and book professionals, chat, pay into escrow, receive deliverables and leave reviews — with a full ops/admin console behind it. This walkthrough tours the product using a live, seeded demo dataset centred on photographer Lola Okeke Studios and customer Chidi O.

Overview

Two apps make up the product:

📱 Mobile app Flutter · iOS/Android

One app, two sides. Customers discover, book, pay via escrow, chat and review; professionals get a dashboard, quote requests, package management and insights. This is where the rich media lives.

🛠️ Admin console Next.js · Web

Operations back-office: users, bookings, feed moderation, reviews, payments, payouts, disputes, ledger, FX, subscriptions and concierge.

Customer app — Flutter (iOS)

The consumer experience where customers discover professionals, book, chat, pay and review. Real photos and video render in the discovery feed and portfolios.

All screens below are live captures from the app running on Android against the seeded backend, shown in both light and dark themes.

Sign-in

Email/password with Google & Apple sign-in.

Discovery feed

Customers browse showcase posts — real photos & video — filterable by category (Photography, Video, DJs, Design).

Post detail & professional profile

Tapping a post opens the work with escrow-safe "Request a quote"; the profile shows ratings, followers, reply time, rebook rate and a Work / Packages / Reviews / About portfolio.

Bookings & escrow

Active vs. completed bookings with lifecycle states — "Funds secured" (in escrow) and "Awaiting payment" — and a booking detail explaining that funds release only on approval.

In-app chat

Per-booking messaging (with call & video) and an escrow-protection reminder to keep chat & payments on-platform.

Professional app — the creator side

The same app, signed in as a professional (Lola Okeke Studios), surfaces a creator dashboard: earnings & escrow, incoming quote requests, package management, and performance insights. Live Android captures in light and dark.

Onboarding & welcome

First-run category tour and a welcome screen leading to sign-in or sign-up.

Creator dashboard

Available balance with a Withdraw action, funds held in escrow "releasing soon", KPI tiles (new requests, active jobs, rating) and a "Next up" action list (respond to requests, deliver files).

Jobs — quotes & active work

Incoming quote requests to respond to, plus active jobs with escrow / review / dispute states.

Manage packages

The creator's services with pricing and completion modes — fixed packages and custom-quote — editable inline.

Insights

Account

Admin / Ops console — Next.js web

The operational back-office. Every figure below is a live screenshot rendering the seeded dataset.

Sign-in & overview

Password login with WebAuthn / passkey support; a KPI overview lands you in the console.

People & commerce

Manage users and follow every booking through its lifecycle (quote → escrow → delivery → completed).

Content moderation

Approve or reject showcase posts for the discovery feed; moderate ratings and written reviews.

Money movement

Escrow payments, professional payouts, and a double-entry ledger with multi-currency FX.

Trust, safety & growth

Dispute resolution queue, subscription plans, and concierge requests.
